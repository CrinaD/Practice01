Tasty Omelet

What to do

  1. Take a pan, put 1/2 tbsp coconut oil in pan
  2. Put the eggs in the pan and make omelet
  3. Put it on a plate together with the cheese, olives, avocado and tomato
  4. Add salt
  5. Enjoy! :)

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • Coconut Oil
  • Cheese
  • Olives
  • 1/2 Avocado
  • 1 tomato
