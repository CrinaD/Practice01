Shrimp Salad

What to do

  1. Put Shrimp in the oven util properly cooked
  2. When ready, take out of oven and add lime juice
  3. Put it on a plate togheter with boiled asparagus.tomato and lettuce and broccoli
  4. Add salt
  5. Enjoy! :)

Ingredients

  • Shrimp
  • Coconut Oil
  • Asparagus
  • Iceberg lettuce
  • Broccoli
  • 1 tomato
  • Lime Juice
